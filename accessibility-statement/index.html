Accessibility Statement

PDDG aims to provide an inclusive and usable website experience.

PDDG aims to provide a website that is usable by people with a range of abilities, devices, and assistive technologies.

Design approach

The redesign uses semantic headings, keyboard-accessible navigation, visible focus states, labelled controls, text alternatives for meaningful images, responsive layouts, and support for reduced-motion preferences.

Ongoing improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. Content, browser behavior, assistive technologies, and implementation may change over time, and issues may still occur.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, contact Info@pddg.io or 301-532-1463 and describe the page, issue, assistive technology, and preferred response method when practical.
