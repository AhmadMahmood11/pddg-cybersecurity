Assessment coverage

Coverage shaped around the approved objective.

Testing never extends beyond explicitly authorized systems, facilities, methods, identities, locations, or time windows.

Approved coverage.

Methodology

Structured, authorized, and grounded in business context.

The process adapts to the selected service and environment. No active testing begins without an agreed scope and rules of engagement.

Service questions

Common questions about penetration testing.

Testing is planned to reduce unnecessary operational risk, but no active test is entirely risk-free. Rules of engagement, exclusions, escalation paths, and testing windows are confirmed before work begins.

Retesting may be included depending on the agreed scope. The proposal should state the retest window, eligible findings, and reporting method.

A scan identifies potential weaknesses. A penetration test adds human validation and controlled exploitation to understand whether and how those weaknesses can be used.
