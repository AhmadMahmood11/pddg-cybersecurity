Our purpose

Make security evidence useful to the people responsible for action.

PDDG focuses on authorized assessment, defensible evidence, business context, practical remediation guidance, and reporting that supports technical and executive decisions.

01 · Independence

Evidence before assumption.

Findings are framed around observed conditions, approved validation, stated limitations, and the decision the engagement must support.

02 · Restraint

Testing without unnecessary risk.

Authorization, exclusions, safe methods, stop conditions, escalation, and evidence handling are established before active work.

03 · Action

Recommendations that fit reality.

Priorities account for exploitability, business impact, operational constraints, ownership, dependencies, and remediation effort.

Expertise

Coverage across connected control layers.

Organizations are exposed through systems, applications, people, policies, facilities, wireless environments, vendors, and the decisions connecting them.
