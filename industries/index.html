Cross-sector principle

Protect the mission while evaluating the controls.

Methods, scheduling, evidence, and escalation must fit the environment. Assessment supports decision-making; it does not guarantee compliance or eliminate risk.

01 · Authorization

Define the boundary before testing.

Assets, facilities, methods, identities, locations, time windows, exclusions, and stop conditions must be explicit.

02 · Safeguards

Respect operational constraints.

Testing methods, evidence, escalation, and scheduling should protect critical operations and sensitive information.

03 · Decisions

Frame findings for action.

Connect evidence to business impact, accountable ownership, remediation sequence, and validation expectations.
