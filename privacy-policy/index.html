This policy explains how information submitted through or associated with the PDDG website may be handled.

Information you provide

You may provide contact information and a general description of your inquiry when contacting PDDG. Do not submit credentials, vulnerability details, protected information, regulated data, or sensitive operational information through a public website form.

In this static redesign, form submissions are prepared in your local email application and are not stored by the website itself. Your email provider and PDDG email systems may process the message you choose to send.

Technical information

The hosting platform and ordinary internet infrastructure may process technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, requested pages, timestamps, and security logs. PDDG does not use this website to intentionally collect sensitive assessment evidence.

How information may be used

Information may be used to respond to inquiries, evaluate service fit, maintain business records, protect systems, meet legal obligations, and improve communications. Information is not presented as being sold to advertisers.

Security and retention

Reasonable safeguards may be used, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed secure. Retention depends on business, legal, security, and operational requirements.
